Reduced cohomology viewer

Displays the cohomology of the graph complex hat X from (PayneWillwacher2021). The complex consists of graphs with hairs labelled \(\epsilon\) or \(\omega\), or numbered. Computes the weight two compactly supported cohomology of the moduli spaces of curves \(\mathcal M_{g,n}\) for \((g,n)\neq (1,1)\).
